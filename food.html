Sushi Maruyama

Sushi Maruyama is the Japanese restaurant I chose to eat twice. The dining room is small in size. There is a small bar at the entrance. Guests can choose to sit at the bar and watch chefs make Sushi. The service at this restaurant is very good, they have the omakase for every day.

This rice don comes with grilled salmon, uni and salmon roe of the day we were there. The creamy texture is complemented by the fragrant rice. A day's satisfaction.

The three kinds of Nigiri on the picture were the ones we ordered separately. Salmon was rich in fat, a satisfying bite. Sweet prawns were sweet and crisp. Tuna is good too, but SALMON is my favorite.
